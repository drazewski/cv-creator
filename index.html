About the product

A free resume builder for people who value clarity, speed and privacy.

If you are looking for a free CV builder, an online resume tool or a simple CV generator, MyCeeVee was designed exactly for that purpose.

The app lets you create a professional CV online without complicated desktop software and without creating an account. In one place you can edit your summary, experience, education, skills, languages, certifications and custom sections, while seeing the result immediately in a print-ready A4 layout.

Unlike many generic form-based editors, this CV builder gives you a few concrete ways to edit. You can choose between two core layouts, including an ATS-optimized single-column resume, change fonts and colours, manage section visibility and tailor the document to a specific application. That makes the tool useful both as an online resume builder and as a practical CV creator for European and international markets.

Because the free version works directly in the browser and does not require login for normal use, it is a very good option for people who want to make a CV quickly, improve an existing resume or generate a polished PDF before sending an application.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is this CV builder free?

Yes. The free version is meant to be fully useful on its own and does not require an account for normal editing.

Can I prepare a CV as a PDF?

Yes. The editor is built around print-ready CV output, and export is being developed to support both printing and direct PDF download.

Is the single-column resume ATS-friendly?

Yes. The US Resume layout keeps the structure simple, uses standard section headings and avoids multi-column blocks, which makes it a better fit for ATS parsing than more decorative layouts.

Can I use the app in different languages?

Yes. English is the default language, and the interface already supports many languages. The landing page is also prepared for multilingual use.

Do I need to sign up to use the app?

No. You can use the free version of the app without registering or logging in.
